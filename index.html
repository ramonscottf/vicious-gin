Meet Kara

A lifetime of
roots in Davis County.

Born in Vernal and raised in Davis County since age three, Kara Toone has spent her entire life here — attending Viewmont High, earning degrees from Snow College and Weber State University, and completing a Master's in Public Policy from the University of Utah.

She and her husband Logan have lived in Clearfield since 2003 — more than two decades raising their four children in the same neighborhood. As Executive Director of Davis Education Foundation, she works daily to ensure every student in the district has the support they need.

Kara has been active in the Republican Party at every level — chairing county conventions, serving on the State Central Committee, and working as a county and state delegate. She's not new to public service. She's built for it.

Five Priorities for
House District 14

Real solutions, grounded in data, driven by community values.

01

Growth & Infrastructure

Transportation · Utilities · Planning
Clearfield and Syracuse are growing fast, and infrastructure must keep pace. I'll advocate for coordinated transportation, housing, and land-use decisions aligned with WFRC's Unified Transportation Plan — ensuring roads, transit, and public services grow with our community, not years behind it.
+
02

Housing Affordability & Neighborhood Stability

Homeownership · Local Control · Permitting
This is personal. My own young adult children wonder if they can afford to stay in the community where they grew up. I'll support practical, Utah-based solutions that make homeownership attainable for the next generation — expanding starter homes, cutting red tape, and maintaining strong local control.
+
03

Safe Communities & Law Enforcement

Public Safety · First Responders · Trust
Safe neighborhoods don't happen by accident. I'll champion investments in officer recruitment and retention, mental-health support for first responders, modern equipment, and community-focused policing strategies that build trust between residents and the men and women who protect them.
+
04

Strong Schools & Support for Families

Education · Funding · Accountability
As someone who works directly in public education, I see the complexities of budgeting, staffing, and enrollment shifts firsthand. Any adjustment to tax structure must provide reliable, predictable funding for students — and all taxpayer-supported programs must be held to equal standards of transparency and accountability.
+
05

Fiscal Responsibility & Local Control

Budgeting · Transparency · Government
Taxpayers deserve leaders who respect their hard-earned dollars. I'll work to strengthen transparency, reduce unnecessary spending, and support policies that allow cities, counties, and school districts to govern effectively — without overreach from the state. Efficient. Responsive. Locally focused.
+
