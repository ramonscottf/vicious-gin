Questions?

We get asked these a lot.

How much does it cost?
Every return is different, so we quote based on complexity. Simple individual returns are very affordable, and we're always transparent about pricing before we start. No surprises, ever. Reach out for a free quote!
What do I need to bring?
Your W-2s, any 1099 forms, last year's tax return (if you have it), a photo ID, and Social Security numbers for you and dependents. If you're self-employed, bring income and expense records. Don't worry if you're missing something — we'll walk you through it.
Can I file online without coming in?
Absolutely! You can upload your documents through our secure client portal and we'll handle everything remotely. We also do video calls if you want that face-to-face experience from home. Or just drop off your docs — whatever works for you.
I haven't filed in years. Can you help?
Yes — and please don't feel embarrassed. It's more common than you think, and we specialize in tax resolution. We'll help you get caught up, negotiate with the IRS if needed, and get you back on track. Zero judgment, guaranteed.
Do you help with small businesses?
It's one of our favorite things to do! We serve over 1,000 small businesses every year. From sole proprietors to LLCs and S-Corps, we'll handle your business taxes, bookkeeping, and help you plan for growth. We love helping local businesses thrive.
